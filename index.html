StringerVision™ Clients

Stringers are like Uber drivers but for remote eyes, a part of the new sharing economy. What StringerVision shares is video information - the what, when, and where is wanted by our Clients.

When a Client wants to generate some video content at a particular location, we provide a map with pins dropped for Stringers located nearby. Only Stringers who are ready for work (those with their GPS turned on) are mapped. When the Client taps on a pin, the Stringer's hourly rate appears - together with a link to the Stringer's portfolio of work, the Stringer's terms of service and levels of technology, and a link to call the Stringer.

PayPal facilitates the immediate transfer of fund from Clients to Stringers. They also accrue the small commission paid to StringerVision for this service.

Terms of Service

There are three future profit-sharing options:

  • Stringer keeps the future rights to the work. Client gets a non-exclusive use option.
  • Stringer and Client both share the future rights.
  • Client owns the future rights.
The hourly rates for these three options will vary. For example, A: $30/hour, B: $40/hour, C: $50/hour

Hiring a Stringer

The Client simply enters the number of hours into the StringerVision app and clicks Hire. PayPal facilitates the immediate transfer of fund from Clients to Stringers. They also accrue the small commission paid to StringerVision for this service. The Stringer immediately receives one-third of the payment in the Stringer's PayPal account. The next third is paid automatically when the Stringer clicks on Work Complete in the app. The final third is paid when the Client receives the video and clicks one of the satisfaction levels. Satisfaction reports are used to establish the Stringer's online reputation.
